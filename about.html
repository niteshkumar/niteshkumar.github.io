I am a digital product designer, currently working at HackerEarth. My skill set includes information architecture, interaction design, interface prototyping and visual design. I have a strong understanding of user research and front-end development.

As a self-starter, I started designing interfaces when I was an undergraduate at IIT Roorkee. Since then, I have been designing full-time leading end-to-end design process, from conception to launch. My design process ranges from broad system-level thinking to crafting minute interactions in pixel-perfect detail.

I am passionate about solving complex problems that go beyond just the visual interface. I love working with interdisciplinary teams, learning something new while doing it and making a positive impact.

When I'm not designing, you can find me taking photos, writing or listening to music.
